Percentage of Vegans & Vegetarians by country

The first visualization is a line graph that shows the percentage of vegans and vegetarians by country. This graph allows us to compare how various nations around the globe have adopted plant-based diets. By plotting both vegan and vegetarian percentages side-by-side, we can observe how dietary trends differ and where plant-based lifestyles are gaining the most traction. Each point on the graph represents a country, and the data highlights the growing global interest in reducing animal product consumption.

Plant-Based Product Sales Growth

Our bar graph presents a closer look at the sales and projected growth of plant-based products. This chart compares current sales figures to the forecasted market expansion, giving a glimpse into the increasing consumer demand for plant-based meat, dairy alternatives, beverages, and other products. The data reflects the significant economic impact of the plant-based sector, which continues to grow as more consumers shift towards sustainable and ethical eating habits.
