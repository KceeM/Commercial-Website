The Purpose of the following Data Visualisations:

The data visualizations on this page offer insights into the global trends and metrics related to plant-based lifestyles. The bubble graph provides an interactive way to explore data on plant-based product attributes, such as price and health scores, sourced from manual entries inspired by market insights.
