My Journey to Plant-Based Living

In 2020, during the global pandemic caused by COVID-19—a highly contagious virus that led to respiratory illnesses, hospitalizations, and millions of deaths worldwide—I became increasingly concerned about my health...

In March 2020, I decided to take action by becoming pescatarian... I knew I had to make this change for my health.

As the pandemic worsened, so did my worries. In June 2020, my grandfather passed away, and soon after, I contracted COVID-19... My mom returned from the hospital, and I knew from that moment on I would commit fully to this lifestyle.

Going vegan felt like a natural transition... Living in South Africa, where most people don’t understand or support plant-based living, hasn’t been easy. Many people judge my choices, but I’ve remained steadfast.

...Even small changes, like having meatless meals a few times a week, can significantly impact your health and the planet...

Your body will thank you later!
